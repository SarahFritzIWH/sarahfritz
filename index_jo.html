Johanna Raith

Hello! My name is Johanna Raith and I am a PhD student at the Halle Institute for Economic Research (IWH).

My research interests are education and labor economics with a focus on higher education choices and preference formation. In my current projects I am investigating drivers of educational and occupational choices such as beliefs on admission probability, geographic preferences and social or gender norms.

Besides that, I am particularly interested in topics related to equality of opportunity, gender and culture.
